- from Greek diabetes mellitus:
- diabetes: to pass through or siphon
- mellitus: honey, sweet
- Types of diabetes
  - Type 1
    - insulin-deficiency
    - immune system destroys [[#Insulin][Insulin]] producing cells in the pancreas
  - Type 2
    - [[#Insulinresistenz][Insulin-resistance]]
    - [[#Glukose][Glucose]] cannot enter muscle cells
    - fat seems to block the insulin-signaling process
