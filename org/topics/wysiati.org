- *What You See Is All There Is*
- is a phenomenon that causes us to focus on existing evidence and ignore absent evidence
  - [[#Thinking%2C%20Fast%20and%20Slow%20%2F%20System%201][Thinking, Fast and Slow / System 1]] will create a coherent story based on limited evidence
  - these impressions/intuitions can be endorsed by [[#Thinking%2C%20Fast%20and%20Slow%20%2F%20System%202][Thinking, Fast and Slow / System 2]]
    - they will become values and beliefs
  - that's why people jump to conclusions, assume bad intentions, are "victims" of biases and buy into conspirational theories
- Problems
  - [[#Factfulness%2FGeneralization%20instinct][Factfulness/Generalization instinct]]
    - people don't really understand statistics
    - they take small numbers and conclude that's representative for the whole population
    - if one personally observes "one" incident, he/she is more likely to think the same incident might apply to the whole population
      - Racism
      - Terrorism especially in combination with [[#Start%20with%20Why%20-%20How%20Great%20Leaders%20Inspire%20Everyone%20To%20Take%20Action%20-%20Note%204][fear]]
