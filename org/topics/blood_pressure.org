- 1st number (systolic): represents the pressure in the arteries
- 2nd number (diastolic): pressure in arteries when heart is resting between beats
- normal values: 120/80
- hypertension > 140/90
- Against hypertension
  - flax seeds
  - hibiscus tea
  - nitrate-rich vegetables
